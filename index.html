B. Pharm Student | Computational Biology & Molecular Dynamics

I am an undergraduate Pharmacy student at Islamic University, Kushtia (CGPA: 3.83/4.00, Class Rank: 2nd). I am deeply passionate about computational structural biology, protein dynamics, and neurodegenerative diseases (ALS, Alzheimer's). My research utilizes advanced MD simulations (minimum 1 microsecond to infer accurate protein folding) to study mutational effects on protein complexes. My long-term goal is to pursue a fully-funded PhD in virotherapy or cellular biology and establish an independent research laboratory.
